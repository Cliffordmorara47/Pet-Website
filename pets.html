In our everyday life, we come across animals that we would love to become part of our lives and others that are feared because of their reputation. But in any way it is important to understand that some of these animals need our care and protection. Some pets are even put up for adoption, you can click here to read more on that.

Some of the famous pets we have amidst us include;

  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Horse

Here are some fun facts about these animals that probably you didn't know;

Dogs

  • Dogs noses are wet to help absorb scent chemicals.
  • Three dogs survived the Titanic sinking.
  • A Bloodhound's sense of smell can be used as evidence in court.

Cats

  • The oldest known pet cat existed 9,500 years ago
  • Cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping
  • The record for the longest cat ever is 48.5 inches

Horses

  • Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up.
  • Horses can run shortly after birth.
  • Domestic horses have a lifespan of around 25 years.
